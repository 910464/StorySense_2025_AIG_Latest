ID: BRLLR13-US001
Description: Display of the CMP indicator on the Submission Summary Screen
AcceptanceCriteria: 
1. Given a user (Shared Services/Operations/UW Support) logs into DWF
2. And create/opens a NB or renewal submission record from My List on the submission work queue (applicable for all products including NA AL/GL/WC, EU other products & NA FL)
3. When user lands on Submission Summary screen
4. Then system displays a Control Master Program field under the insured section on the submission screen
